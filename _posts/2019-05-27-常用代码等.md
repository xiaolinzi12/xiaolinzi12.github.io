### mysql获取所有表名

````
SELECT
	TABLE_NAME
FROM
	INFORMATION_SCHEMA.TABLES
WHERE
	TABLE_SCHEMA = 'TABLE_SCHEMA_NAME'
AND 
	TABLE_TYPE = 'BASE TABLE';
*	TABLE_SCHEMA_NAME : 库名         
````
